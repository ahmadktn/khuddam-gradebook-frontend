  About the College

A Legacy of Health Education in Northern Nigeria

Khuddam College of Health Science and Technology (KCHST) is a foremost institution dedicated to the training of middle-level health professionals in Nigeria. Located in Katsina State, the College has consistently produced graduates who serve in hospitals, primary health care centres, and research institutions nationwide.

Accredited by the National Board for Technical Education (NBTE) and affiliated with relevant professional regulatory bodies, KCHST upholds the highest standards in health sciences education, combining rigorous academic instruction with hands-on clinical training.
